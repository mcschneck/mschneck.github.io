SELECTED PROJECTS

REPO

This project is a small image processing library that utilizes openCV to add and remove alpha channels of images. One way to use this library is to remove all the white space in a logo and make it transparent.

This was a year long research project centered around the relationship between users and their hardwear. Specifically, we wanted to teach users how to fix their own devices and empower them to make more sustainable choices surrounding technology.
